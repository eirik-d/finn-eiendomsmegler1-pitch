AI Summary of Property Listing and Condition Report

A collaboration between FINN Real Estate and Aktiv Eiendomsmegling

The marketplace of opportunities

The Situation

Home buyers expect better assistance

FINN is the most important showcase for residential properties. Buyers start their home search with us and increasingly expect better help understanding the information before making major decisions.

Today, buyers must navigate to the property's website themselves, download the sales prospectus and condition report, and interpret complex documents on their own.

The Threat

Third-party services already offer AI summaries of property information — completely outside the control of agencies and agents.

Growing Adoption

Usage of such services is increasing. Buyers receive summarised information without quality assurance or any connection to the agent.

Lost Control

Without a quality-assured alternative, both the agency and FINN lose the opportunity to shape the buyer's first impression.

User Flow

Benefits

What Aktiv gains from the collaboration

01

Traffic Preservation

The sales prospectus remains Aktiv's. FINN does not replace the agent's own pages, but serves as an effective gateway to them.

02

Higher Quality Leads

Buyers who already understand the risks and key facts about a property are more ready to make decisions when they contact the agent.

03

Fewer Disputes and Complaints

When buyers have the right expectations early on, the risk of post-sale disputes and complaints is significantly reduced.

04

Clear Positioning

Collaborating on transparency and better buyer guidance strengthens Aktiv's reputation as a professional and innovative agency.

The Solution

AI summary directly in the FINN listing

Highlights surfaced — The property's strengths and opportunities are brought to the buyer's attention

Direct link to Aktiv — A clear CTA sends the buyer to Aktiv's pages for the complete prospectus

Things you should know — Key findings from the condition report presented clearly with detailed explanations

Limited view — FINN only shows the summary — the full prospectus remains with the agency
